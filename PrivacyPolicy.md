# Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 31, 2026

Bot: EarthALiEN Mission Control Bot / @EATMCbot

## Short Version

EATMCbot is designed to avoid collecting personal data.

The bot does not ask for wallet private keys, seed phrases, passwords, payment information, or wallet access. The bot does not sell user data.

## Information The Bot May Receive

When you interact with EATMCbot, Telegram may send the bot basic message and chat information required for the bot to function. This can include:

- Telegram chat IDs, group IDs, user IDs, usernames, and display names.
- Commands and messages sent to the bot.
- Admin-configured token contracts or token addresses.
- Group alert settings.
- Message IDs for bot messages that may need to be edited or deleted to keep the chat clean.
- Public market, token, trade, and blockchain data related to tracked tokens.

## How Information Is Used

Information is used only to operate bot features, including:

- Responding to commands.
- Remembering group token settings.
- Posting token price and buy alerts.
- Keeping the latest alerts visible while removing older bot messages when cleanup is enabled.
- Preventing abuse, diagnosing errors, and maintaining bot reliability.

## What The Bot Does Not Collect

EATMCbot does not intentionally collect:

- Wallet private keys.
- Seed phrases.
- Exchange passwords.
- Payment card information.
- Government ID information.
- Precise location data.
- Contacts outside the chats where the bot is used.

Do not send private keys, seed phrases, passwords, or sensitive personal information to the bot.

## Data Retention

Group settings may be retained until an admin changes or removes them.

Recent alert references, such as bot message IDs used for cleanup, are intended to be limited to the current cleanup window, such as the latest 10 buy alerts.

Temporary operational logs may be kept for reliability and debugging, then deleted or rotated when no longer needed.

## Third-Party Services

The bot operates through Telegram, so Telegram's own terms and privacy practices apply to Telegram messages and accounts.

The bot may also read public token, market, trade, or blockchain data from third-party services or public networks. These services may have their own terms and privacy policies.

## Data Sharing

EATMCbot does not sell user data.

Data may be processed by infrastructure needed to operate the bot, Telegram, and public market/blockchain data providers. Data may also be disclosed if required by law or to protect the bot, users, or service operators from abuse.

## User Controls

You can stop interacting with the bot at any time.

Group admins can remove the bot from a group or change bot settings using available bot commands.

## Crypto Disclaimer

EATMCbot provides informational token activity and price data only. It is not financial advice. Token prices and trade data may be delayed, incomplete, or inaccurate.

## Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the EarthALiENtim community or the operator of @EATMCbot on Telegram.
